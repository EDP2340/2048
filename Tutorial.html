How to play:Swipe your finger across the sensor to move the tile.

When they dial the same number, they merge into one!

2048 is a addictive puzzle game based on simple addition.

The goal of the game is to reach the number 2048

by combining adjacent tiles with the same numbers on a 4x4 square field.

At the beginning of the game, two tiles with the number 2 are available,

which are in a random position on the field, and after each of your moves,

new tiles with the number 2 or 4 appear.

Once you completely fill the field with tiles and

there are no free cells for the turn, the game will end.
